Be an active member
As a member, you’ll take part in workshops, competitions, and events while building valuable marketing and leadership skills. Active engagement is the best way to maximize your experience and opportunities within MarketMinds.
Join our leadership
Auxiliary Board
The Auxiliary Board allows students to step into leadership by assisting officers with recruitment, fundraising, and event planning. Each month, high-performing Auxiliary members are promoted into Officer roles, making this a direct path to greater responsibility. Applications open once a month, so keep an eye out. What we look for:
* Strong communication and collaboration skills
* Reliability and follow-through on commitments
* Creativity and initiative in problem-solving
HS Directory Board
Our Directory Board is the backbone of MarketMinds, composed of dedicated high school leaders who oversee operations and guide middle school officers. To be considered, you must reach out directly to the current Directory Board. What we look for:
* Proven leadership and organizational skills
* Experience in marketing, sales, or event management
* Commitment to growing MarketMinds’ impact locally and nationally
