Overview of 2025 Events

In 2025, MarketMinds is set to host a full slate of workshops, seminars, field trips, and fundraising initiatives designed to sharpen marketing and sales skills. From interactive guest speakers to hands-on competitions, students will gain real-world experiences that extend far beyond the classroom.

Open House

Our first-ever Open House marked the official kickoff of the 2025–26 school year. Attendees participated in engaging, business-themed workshops while learning about MarketMinds’ mission and opportunities. The event also introduced our new Auxiliary Board, giving students a pathway to leadership and involvement. With strong energy and participation, the Open House set the tone for an exciting year ahead.

BasketBall Fundraiser

MarketMinds hosted its first Basketball Fundraiser at Fowler Park, where members competed in fast-paced 3v3 teams. Sponsored by Chick-fil-A and featuring a $100 cash prize, the event raised $355 to support future initiatives. This tournament marked the beginning of many creative fundraisers that will help fuel MarketMinds’ growth and community impact.

Purpose of Pillars Camp

The Pillars Camp was created to give middle school students a strong foundation in marketing and sales before they enter high school. Each session focuses on core pillars—knowing your audience, branding and storytelling, and promotion and advertising—through hands-on activities and workshops. The camp not only builds essential business skills but also helps students gain confidence, creativity, and leadership experience at an early age. By the end of the camp, participants leave with practical knowledge they can apply to future school clubs, competitions, and personal projects.

Guest Speaker Event: Michael Hall

Our next featured event will host Mr. Hall, an International Business teacher at Alliance Academy for Innovation, who will share global perspectives and practical strategies with students. This event marks the start of an exciting guest speaker series.
