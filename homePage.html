Overview

MarketMinds is a high school–led nonprofit dedicated to preparing middle school students for the future through hands-on marketing, sales, and leadership experiences. We stand for creativity, confidence, and real-world readiness, empowering students to think like innovators and act like leaders.

Our Story
Founded in Forsyth County in the summer of 2025, MarketMinds was created to reduce the friction students face when transitioning into high school and preparing for college. What began with a single idea has scaled to 40+ active members in just three months. Today, our leadership includes 18 students across high school and middle school, serving on the High School Directory Board, Middle School Officer Board, and Middle School Auxiliary Board.

Structure

Events
MarketMinds offers a wide range of experiences, including interactive workshops, chapter competitions, guest speaker seminars, educational field trips, and engaging fundraisers. Every event is designed to connect theory with practice, ensuring students develop meaningful skills while having fun.
Leadership Team
Our multi-layered leadership model gives students opportunities at every level. The High School Directory Board sets vision and strategy, while Middle School Officers lead chapters, and Auxiliary Board members gain hands-on training. This structure allows students to grow step by step into confident leaders.
Partners
We are proud to be supported by professionals and companies that bring real-world expertise to our students. Partners like Chick-fil-A and Chesstronics, create a meaningful impact along with our organization.
Advisors
Multiple local educators and business leaders, provide mentorship, sponsorship, and valuable industry insights. THis greatly raises the value of our team and the impact we create on students.
